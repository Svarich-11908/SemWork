MovieDB: the complete cinematography online museum

The eras from renaissance to transavant-garde. The site contains movies by the most famous directors, presents data on painting museums and art galleries around the world. You can also familiarize yourself with the information of the artist you are interested in, information about their works. The date of creation, information about contents is expected. The gallery now features screenshots from the most popular movies and is one of the most complete virtual art museums. The museum covers the history of cinematography around the world and the famous movie shots in particular. The history of Russian movies is presented not only by the famous directors' works, but also by the directions of symbolism, avant-garde, post-impressionism, as well as romanticism, modernism and impressionism (it's not presented, actually)
